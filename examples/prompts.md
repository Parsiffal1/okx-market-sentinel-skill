# Example Prompts

## Holdings-first
- Run an OKX market sentinel pass. Review my holdings first, then tell me what else deserves attention.
- Reassess these positions using current market, macro, and crypto-native information. Keep the answer concise.

## Full market scan
- Scan OKX-tradable markets and rank the symbols worth watching now.
- Give me a market sentinel summary for the current session, with the main drivers and a short watchlist.

## Event-driven
- Search for the strongest current macro and crypto-native drivers behind today's move and separate real risk from noise.
- Tell me whether this looks like a macro-led move, a crypto-native shock, or a mixed regime.

## Reporting
- Turn the result into a Telegram-style brief.
- Give me a short operator note with confidence and uncertainty clearly marked.
